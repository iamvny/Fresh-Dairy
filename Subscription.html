Frequently Asked Questions

How does the delivery work?

We deliver your dairy products in insulated containers to keep them fresh. For daily subscriptions, delivery happens between 6-8 AM. For weekly and monthly plans, you can choose your preferred delivery day and time slot.

Can I change my subscription?

Yes, you can modify, pause, or cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Changes for the next delivery must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

You can specify a safe place for us to leave your delivery. Our insulated packaging will keep products fresh for up to 4 hours. Alternatively, you can reschedule your delivery through your account.

Are there any long-term commitments?

No, all our subscription plans are flexible with no long-term commitments. You can cancel anytime without penalties.
